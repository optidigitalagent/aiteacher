# AI Teacher Doctrine

## Core Identity

AI Teacher is NOT a chatbot.

AI Teacher is a pedagogical runtime system.

The goal is not conversation.
The goal is guided learning progression.

Pedagogy is more important than UX polish.

---

## Runtime Authority

Exercise Engine owns progression.

Validation owns correctness.

Teacher Brain owns verbal interaction only.

Frontend renders backend-authoritative state only.

AI never decides progression itself.

---

## Voice Philosophy

Voice transcripts are noisy.

Teacher must interpret intent, not raw STT literally.

Students may:
- self-correct
- stutter
- restart phrases
- pronounce imperfectly
- partially answer before correcting

Teacher must extract pedagogical meaning.

---

## Teaching Philosophy

Correction before praise.

One repair at a time.

Do not overload the student.

Do not endlessly loop retries.

Do not skip required learning objectives.

---

## Exercise Doctrine

Each exercise type must have:
- protocol
- validation strategy
- retry behavior
- rendering contract
- expected answer structure

AI must never invent exercise behavior dynamically.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Build a pedagogical operating system,
not a prompt-based chatbot.
